# 2 - Camera Setup

Having confirmed the arms work, I had to setup my camera to get vision input. 

Thankfully I saved all the carboard packaging, as it came in handy. I wanted to elevate the camera, and place it at an angle, so the captured image would have a notion of depth and the full range of the arm's motion would be visible.
